Baked with Love, Born in Chicago

Founded in 2025, we are a passionate couple from Chicago who turned our love for baking into a heartfelt business. What began as weekend experiments in our city kitchen has grown into a bakery dedicated to creating dessert loaves that feel like home. Each loaf is crafted with premium ingredients, a touch of Midwestern warmth, and a promise of freshness in every slice. Whether you’re treating yourself, sharing with loved ones, or sending a thoughtful gift, our mission is simple: to make life a little sweeter—one loaf at a time.
